Pentashooter 2D Instructions:
- Press P to play
- Move using W, A, S, D
- Move your camera using U, H, J, K
- Press SPACE to shoot
- Hold G to turn small. You cannot shoot while small, but you move at 3x speed
- You have 3 lives. You lose lives by touching enemies
- Every time you move, the enemies have a chance to move too
- Shoot enemies to destroy them. Red enemies have 3 lives, purple enemies have 6 lives
- Powerups are hidden in some clouds
- Shoot a Powerup to become invinsible for a short time
- You cannot shoot or turn small while invinsible, but you can ram enemies instead
- Avoid the red boundaries, they will hurt you
- Press ESC to reset the game
